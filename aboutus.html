Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about inspections, payments, and warranty.

Do you do site inspections before installation?

Yes. We offer free site inspections in Accra and nearby areas to assess gate size, power access, terrain, and security requirements.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money and bank transfers. For larger projects, milestone payments are available.

What is your warranty?

Installations include up to 2 years warranty, covering workmanship and manufacturer defects under normal use.

How long does installation take?

Typical single-gate automation completes within 1–2 days after materials are on-site; fences and multi-system jobs may take longer.
